Time Wealth: FREEDOM

Time is wealth! If you are a millioner but no time for yourself, you are not wealthy. Manage your time for your work, hobby, family & friends. Your life time is limited to an average of 80 years. Make sure that every day is your own day. Get enough time to sleep, to eat, to play and to work.
