The Physical Wealth: HEALTH

Your health is your wealth! Take a maximum care of your inner universe! Know your body and listen to your feelings! Your health depends on what you eat, drink, and breath. It depends also on how you move and interact with your surrounding environment.Taking care of yourself is the starting point for a wealthy life. This is both physical and emotional.

Here are some suggestions that will help for your physical wealth (health):

  1. Food: Eat more vegetables and fruits, low fat diary products, and whole grain foods.
  2. Drink: Drink lots of water but limited alchol. Pleas do not drink any types of smoke but take fresh oxygen!
  3. Fitness: Move your body! Your endurance is your guard. Walk, run, swim, play football & tennis, ride byscle, build your body, squash, etc.
  4. Safety First: Protect your body from geo-hazards by traveling to safe places. Be safe from car or plane accidents by traveling less & less.
  5. Socializing: Be yourself in all situation. Treat others with respect and you will get it back. Sleep well! Maintain friendship and networks. Very important, Make Love but stay away from war! If you find yourself in the middle of a battlefield, run or surrender! Do not kill animals!
